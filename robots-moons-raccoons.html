WITH BLENDER.

Robots Moons Raccoons is a 3D animated series made primarily in Blender. Using Blender's library system, complex scenes and characters are efficiently reused across multiple files. Blender's rigify addon allows for characters to be quickly rigged and posed naturally.

With Blender's EEVEE real-time rendering engine, scenes can be handsomely rendered in stunning detail without render farms.

IT ALL STARTED WITH DR. HERBAL

This man sent robots to the moons of Saturn to mine rocks for 40 years, intending to sell them later.

What he did not expect was what happened back at home in the meantime.
